Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how iDrop Ultra collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Color palettes and related metadata

Automatically collected information:

  • Anonymous user identifier
  • App usage and performance data
  • Device and system information (non-identifiable)

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality
  • Sync data across devices (Premium)
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Manage subscriptions

3. AI Processing

When using AI features, prompts may be sent to an external AI service solely for generating color palettes. Data is not used for advertising or profiling.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Apple StoreKit

5. Data Security

Data is stored securely on your device and encrypted in transit when synced. While we take reasonable measures, no system is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

Data remains on your device unless deleted. Cloud data is retained while your account is active.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only as necessary to operate the App or comply with legal obligations.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us.

9. Children’s Privacy

iDrop Ultra is not intended for children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page.
